What The Data Shows

Same peptide.
Better results with movement.

Your GLP-1 patients keep asking the same thing: is the peptide doing all the work, or does movement still matter? Fair question, since they're paying a lot for it and want to know what else they can control.

A new network meta-analysis pooled nine randomized trials and 1,009 adults with overweight or obesity, comparing GLP-1 alone, structured exercise alone, and the two together. The combination won on weight, fat mass, waist-to-hip ratio, insulin sensitivity, and HDL cholesterol, beating either approach on its own.

"Combined GLP-1 RA and structured exercise interventions improved weight, insulin sensitivity, and selected lipid parameters more than monotherapies or placebo."
